Placements

GITAM has established training & placement centre in each of the institutes headed by a senior faculty member.
The objectives of the centre are to:

  • Provide training for development of integrated personality.
  • Provide employable skills to all students.
  • Develop innovative entrepreneurs and dynamic leaders for the nation.
  • Achieve 100% gainful placement.

The centre functions under the guidance of the principal of each institute. The training and placement officer is assisted by assistant training officer.
A senior faculty member from each department coordinates the activities of the centre with the active involvement of student coordinators.
